Cloud Storage

Google Drive is a free cloud-based storage software that enables users the ability to store and access different types of data online. Google Drive is a programme that runs along side with other programmes ran by Google. These programmes are those such as; Gmail, Android, Chrome, Youtube, Google Analytics and Google+. Google Drive is a website that can be accessed to reveal different forms of information that can be created. This information is either in the format of documentation, which is accessed though Google Docs, presentations and slides, which is obtained for the Google Slides website, or spreadsheets, in the form of Google Spreadsheets.
